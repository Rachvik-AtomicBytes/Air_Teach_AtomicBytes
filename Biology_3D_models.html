Explore the beauty of Biology through these interactive 3-D models

Human Body Model:

The Human Body Model showcases the intricacies of the human anatomy. It includes detailed representations of organs, muscles, and skeletal structures. Explore the complexity and beauty of the human body in this interactive 3D model.

Human Brain Model:

Delve into the wonders of the Human Brain Model, which provides a comprehensive view of the brain's various regions. Learn about the functions of different parts of the brain and gain insights into the complexities of neural networks.

Human Heart Model:

The Human Heart Model allows you to explore the intricacies of the cardiovascular system. Witness the pumping action of the heart and understand the vital role it plays in circulating blood throughout the body.

Human Small and Large Intestine:

Explore the Human Small and Large Intestine Model to understand the digestive process. Witness the journey of food through these vital organs and gain insights into nutrient absorption in the digestive system.

Human Liver:

The Human Liver Model provides a detailed view of this essential organ. Learn about the liver's functions, including detoxification and metabolic processes. Explore the anatomical details that contribute to its crucial role in maintaining overall health.

Human Stomach:

Immerse yourself in the Human Stomach Model to understand the initial stages of digestion. Explore the structural features of the stomach and gain insights into the processes that break down food into nutrients for absorption.

Human Lungs:

The Human Lungs Model offers a detailed look at the respiratory system. Explore the intricate network of airways and alveoli, gaining insights into the crucial role lungs play in the exchange of oxygen and carbon dioxide in the body.

Flowering Plant:

Embark on a journey through the life of a Flowering Plant. Witness the stages of growth, from seed germination to blooming flowers. Explore the intricate structures that contribute to the reproduction and survival of flowering plants.

Rsp2 Amino Acid Sequence:

The Rsp2 Amino Acid Sequence Model provides an in-depth view of the molecular structure. Explore the arrangement of amino acids and gain insights into the building blocks of proteins, essential for various biological processes in living organisms.
